Dr. Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar

Dr. Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar, fondly known as Bada Saheb Ambedkar, was a visionary social reformer and the principal architect of the Indian Constitution. He dedicated his life to fighting social discrimination, promoting equality, and empowering marginalized communities. His ideas on education, social justice, and human rights continue to inspire millions.
